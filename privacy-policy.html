Privacy Policy for UberFoodies Menu Skill

Last Updated: May 22, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the UberFoodies Menu Skill ("we", "our", or "the skill") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Alexa skill. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

The UberFoodies Menu Skill collects minimal information to provide its service:

  • Voice Requests: We receive the text of your voice requests to Alexa (e.g., "What's today's menu?") to understand and respond to your queries.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as which dates are queried most frequently to improve our service.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses.

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide you with UberFoodies menu information
  • Understand your date-specific menu requests
  • Improve the skill's functionality and user experience
  • Generate anonymous usage analytics

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The only sharing that occurs is:

  • Amazon/Alexa: Your voice requests are processed through Amazon's Alexa service as part of normal skill operation
  • AWS Services: We use Amazon Web Services to store menu data and process requests
  • No Marketing: We do not share your information for marketing purposes

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data is stored securely using Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure
  • We use encryption and secure connections for data transmission
  • Access to data is restricted and monitored
  • We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service

6. Children's Privacy

Our skill does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The skill is designed to provide menu information and does not require any personal information from users of any age.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Disable the Skill: You can disable or delete the UberFoodies Menu Skill at any time through your Alexa app
  • Voice History: You can delete your voice recordings through your Amazon account settings
  • Contact Us: You can contact us with any privacy concerns using the information below

8. Amazon Alexa Integration

This skill operates on the Amazon Alexa platform. Amazon's privacy policy also applies to your use of Alexa. You can review Amazon's privacy policy at https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=468496

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

10. International Users

This skill and its data are hosted in the European Union (specifically AWS eu-north-1 region). If you are accessing this skill from outside the EU, please be aware that your information may be processed in the EU.
